John Boles Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

John Boles was born on October 28, 1895 in Greenville, Texas, USA. He was an actor, known for Frankenstein (1931), Stella Dallas (1937) and Curly Top (1935). He was married to Marcelite Dobbs. He died on February 27, 1969 in San Angelo, Texas. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
