Andrade El Idolo vs. Joey Janela Announced For GCW Highest In The Room 2

Andrade El Idolo will face Joey Janela in his first match in a GCW ring.

As previously announced, AEW star Andrade El Idolo will make his Game Changer Wrestling debut at GCW Highest in the Room 2 on December 9. In an update, GCW announced that Andrade will face Joey Janela at the show. Janela is one of the top stars on the independent scene, and he was previously signed to AEW.

Johnny Game Changer (John Hennigan), Taya Valkyrie, Nick Gage, Matt Cardona, and other noteworthy names are also set for GCW Highest in the Room 2.

Andrade El Idolo is also set to compete at the GCW events on January 12 and January 26.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available, and we will have coverage of GCW Highest in the Room 2 once it airs on December 9.
