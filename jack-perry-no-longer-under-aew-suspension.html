Jack Perry No Longer Under AEW Suspension

Jack Perry known far and wide as ‘Jungle Boy,’ is making some trademark moves that have fans buzzing with anticipation.

Yesterday, Perry took a leap and officially filed for trademark protection for his beloved ‘Jungle Boy’ nickname. But that’s not all—this trademark covers a wide range of merchandise, from hats to shirts, bandanas, sweatshirts, and hooded sweatshirts. It’s clear that Jungle Boy is gearing up to leave his mark not just in the ring but also in the world of fashion and entertainment.

But what about his return to the squared circle? Perry has been conspicuously absent from the AEW scene since August, following a backstage altercation with none other than CM Punk at AEW All In. The wrestling world has been eagerly awaiting his comeback.

In October, rumors started swirling that the AEW roster believed Perry’s suspension had been lifted, but no official confirmation was in sight… until now!

According to Fightful’s Sean Ross Sapp, during a recent Q & A session on Fightful Select, the burning question about Jack Perry’s status was finally addressed. The verdict: Perry is “no longer suspended.”

So, the trademark filing combined with this news has fans speculating that Jungle Boy could be on the brink of making his triumphant return. The jungle drums are beating with excitement, but we’ll have to stay tuned and see if Jack Perry swings back into action anytime soon. The wrestling world awaits!
