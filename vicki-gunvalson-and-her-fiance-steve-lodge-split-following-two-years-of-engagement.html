Vicki Gunvalson And Her Fiance Steve Lodge Split Following Two Years Of Engagement

Vicki Gunvalson and her fiance Steve Lodge called off their engagement after two years, a source confirms to PEOPLE. 

Gunvalson took Instagram to confirm their split, writing, "The time has come for Steve and her to move forward."

The former RHOC (Real Housewives of Orange County) star added that there was no fault with either person. They are just going in different directions, and she wishes him the best life and happiness in his life. She even said that Steve had also expected the same for her.

After their split, the ex-couple who first met at a charity event in 2016, unfollowed each other on Social media. In addition, Vicki has also deleted all of her Instagram photos with Lodge, including their engagement announcement.

E! News was first to report their split. For most of the time on the pandemic, the couple lived separately. While Vicki was in the US, Steve had been living like he was a single man in Puerto Vallarta.

59, Vicki started dating Lodge after her breakup with Brooks Ayers. However, the two of them were together for several years before calling it quit in July 2015. After her love relationship started with Steve, she told PEOPLE that she felt like she had finally found her love of life.

After three years of together, the duo got engaged in April 2019. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the couple decided to postpone their wedding.
