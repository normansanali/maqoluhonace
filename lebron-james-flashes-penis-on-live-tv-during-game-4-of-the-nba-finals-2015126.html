The moment brought back memories of other crazy wardrobe malfunctions broadcast on live TV, including Janet Jackson and Justin Timberlake‘s infamous 2004 nip slip during the Super Bowl Halftime Show. Following the debacle, the FCC slapped CBS with a hefty $550,000 fine for the exposure, although the Supreme Court later voided the charge.

James’ accidental flashing wasn’t his only big moment of the night, though. He also had a hard head-on collision with a camera, which resulted in the player needing stitches. “I had a slight headache, which I think every one of you guys would probably have if you ran into a camera,” James, whose team lost the game on Thursday night, told USA Today. “Like I said, I got a few stitches and I got a slight headache right now but I’ll be fine with that.”
