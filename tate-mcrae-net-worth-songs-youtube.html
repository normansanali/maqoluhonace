Who Is Canadian Dance Sensation Tate McRae? Her Net Worth Details

Tate McRae is a young Canadian Dance sensation, well-acknowledged as one of the finalists of So You Think You Can Dance: The Next Generation. Already a winner of several dance competitions, she started dancing since the age of six.

Along with dancing, she is a singer whose hit song entitled 'Vicious' peaked at number 43 on the Canada CHR/Top 40 chart. See more about her below!!

Who Is Tate McRae?

Tate McRae, age 17, is a Canadian dancer and singer. She was born in 2001 in Calgary, in Canada and she celebrates her birthday on 1 July. McRae is the daughter of Todd Rosner and Tanja Rosner, who grew up with her older brother named Tucker McRae. Her father Todd is a lawyer, and her mother Tanja is a dance instructor while brother Tucker is a hockey player. Tanja Tucker also owns a dance studio.

Concerning her physical attributes, she has a slim body build, and she stands at a height of 5 feet and 7 inches tall and weighs 59 kg or 130 lbs.

How Much Is Tate McRae's Net Worth?

Tate McRae garners her net worth from her professional career as a dancer and a singer. But she has not mentioned any amount of her net worth and fortune.

But she does attain considerable earnings from her YouTube channel. She has a self-titled YouTube channel, which she created back on 27 May 2011. She has 173 uploads on the room that has more than 2.42 million subscribers and 202,598,539 views, as of August 2020.

As per SocialBlade, Tate McRae's YouTube channel earns the monthly income ranging from $1.5K to $24.5K, which makes $18.4K to $294.5K in a year.

Tate McRae became the first runner-up at the New York City Dance Alliance in 2014. She got sponsored by Capezio and trained at the School of Alberta Ballet as well as the YYC Dance Project. She is also the winner of the Mini Best Dancer in NYC at the Dance Awards in 2013. Similarly, in 2016, she became third on the dance reality show, So You Think You Can Dance: The Next Generation.
