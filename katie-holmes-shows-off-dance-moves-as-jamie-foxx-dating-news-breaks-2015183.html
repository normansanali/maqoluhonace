Katie Holmes Shows Off Dance Moves As Jamie Foxx Dating News Breaks

Joey Potter’s got the moves! Katie Holmes was more than happy to show off her impressive dance moves on Wednesday, Mar. 18.

“Kicking into Wednesday!” Holmes, 36, captioned an Instagram video. “Have a great day!”

The grainy video shows Holmes whipping her body to the side and lifting her right leg in the air with her longtime pal Tyce Diorio. The barefoot star is dressed casually in sweatpants and a white tank top, and has her brown locks swept back in a high ponytail. (The actress performed a tribute to Judy Garland on So You Think You Can Dance in July 2009.)

Holmes, a trained dancer, shared the clip just hours after Us Weekly revealed her still-budding romance with old friend Jamie Foxx, 47. The pair have been dating for the past year-and-a-half on the down low.

Dancing, in fact, is how Holmes and Foxx initially hit it off, among other things. (Us broke news back in October 2013 that the Dawson’s Creek alum had cozied up to Foxx.) The Annie actor first wooed Holmes at a Hamptons benefit at the time. They grooved to Robin Thicke’s “Blurred Lines” and Lenny Kravitz’s “American Woman” on stage during the event — and, as it turns out, the pair have been seeing one another ever since.

“Jamie is someone she can trust and have fun with,” a mutual friend recently told Us of Holmes, who shares 8-year-old daughter Suri with ex-husband Tom Cruise. “She’s getting everything from Jamie that she didn’t get with Tom.”
