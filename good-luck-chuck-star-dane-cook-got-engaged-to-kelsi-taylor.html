Good Luck Chuck Star Dane Cook Got Engaged To Kelsi Taylor

Dane Cook, a comedian, and actor, recently proposed to his girlfriend, Kelsi Taylor. Cook proposed to Taylor on July 13 and told People that he was ready to pop the question and that everything, including the weather and sunset, was perfect on the evening of the proposal. Cook went on to say that he was proposing marriage to the lady with whom he had experienced some of the best experiences of his life. Taylor also chipped in, saying:

“My stomach twisted the moment he reached for his back pocket. I couldn’t have wished for a more beautiful setting. All I could think of was, ‘Wait, is this really occurring right now?’ Is he going to propose?'”

She continued, saying:

“I was also really concentrated on what he was saying to me since I was urgently trying to memorize the phrases. I wanted to take it all in because they were so lovely.”

Cook said that he had been preparing the proposal for a long time and chose to do it on their visit to York Beach, Maine, which has been his favorite site since boyhood. Taylor embraced her mother after the proposal and expressed her love for Cook. Dane said that he and Kelsi have great plans and that they want to have a family together.

She then appeared in the comedy series Up in Arms and worked on a couple of short films. She didn’t obtain any more parts, so she concentrated on her singing career. She established a YouTube account and published songs such as Almost Lover and Youth.

Kelsi proceeded to release renditions of additional songs, such as Youth and Don’t Know Why, and in 2018, she published her debut EP, Fear of Yourself. She has performed in a variety of locations and has over 25,000 followers on Instagram. Taylor, who considers herself an introvert, is particularly interested in issues such as psychology. Chopper, her beloved dog, stays with her in Los Angeles.
