Bayley Wants Brock Lesnar To Hit Roman Reigns With Her Finisher

There is no doubt that Bayley is one of the top female Superstars in all of WWE. Her previous reign as Smackdown Women’s Champion lasted 380 days and on top of that, she also has the most days as Women’s Champion in modern WWE. It seems she has a very amusing request for Brock Lesnar as well.

Earlier this year, Bayley suffered a horrific injury while training at the WWE Performance Center. Bayley is expected to be out for 9 months and for that, she underwent knee reconstruction surgery.

On this week’s “Supersized” Friday Night SmackDown, Roman Reigns and Brock Lesnar had the contract signing for their title match at Crown Jewel 2021. The segment ended the show as The Beast had the upper hand, referring to Paul Heyman as “my advocate”.

Fans in attendance made it clear that they wanted to see Brock Lesnar use a german suplex on Roman Reigns as they chanted “Suplex City” during the segment. It seems this also led to a very interesting reaction from Bayley

Bayley took to Twitter and reacted to the segment on Friday Night SmackDown. Bayley stated that she is so mad that she hoped Lesnar gives Roman Reigns a “Bayley to Belly” suplex, which was her finisher.

“I’m so mad that I hope @BrockLesnar gives @WWERomanReigns a Bayley to Belly suplex.”

Ringside News exclusively reported that Roman Reigns and Brock Lesnar are set to have multiple matches this time around. On top of that, their Crown Jewel match is likely to have an interesting finish. We will have to wait and see just how interesting of a finish their Crown Jewel match will have.
