Gigi Hadid Wore My Favorite One-and-Done Fall Layering Piece

As soon as the temperatures drop, I dig through my storage and bring out my cold-weather jackets. I’m talking about puffer jackets, oversized blazers, heavy cardigans, and more. But there’s one silhouette I rely on no matter the season — and Gigi Hadid just wore it on the streets of New York City.

Getting some shopping done, Hadid made an appearance wearing a chocolate-brown leather jacket. The top layer featured an oversized, boxy silhouette, offsetting her flattering black jeans and slim leather loafers. Silver-toned hardware, split lapels, pockets, and a hefty belt completed the separate, sharing many of the same details as this faux leather moto jacket from Levi’s. 

Levi’s Faux Leather Moto Jacket

Lucky Brand Leather Moto Jacket

I’ve always had a thing for leather jackets, wearing them  for more than 11 years. They’re punk-rock meet chic, combining two very different — yet very complementary — styles. Think: Kourtney Kardashian post Travis Barker meets Meghan Markle on a date night. You wouldn’t think the two styles go together, but, boy, do they make for a fashionable yet tasteful combo. 

The merge between the two styles allows me to wear leather jackets with just about anything, including velvet dresses, knee-high boots, button-up shirts, cap-toe ballet flats — the list goes on. They’ve even become my one-and-done top layer of choice, as I always keep a black or brown leather jacket hanging by my door, just within arm’s reach.

Not to mention, Hadid just proved leather jackets can be worn year round; all it takes is a bit of layering. The model made hers extra cozy with a multicolored scarf and Taylor Swift’s 1989 (Taylor’s Version) cardigan. (We love to see besties supporting besties.) You can do the same by layering your own leather jacket, like this Lucky Brand find, over a knit sweater, long-sleeve shirt, or comfortable crewneck.
