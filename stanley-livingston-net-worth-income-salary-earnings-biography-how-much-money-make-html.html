Stanley Bernard Livingston (born November 24, 1950) is an American actor best known for his role as Richard “Chip” Douglas, the third son of Steve Douglas (Fred MacMurray) on the long-running television series My Three Sons. He is the son of Steve Douglas (Fred MacMurray) and the third son of Steve Douglas (Fred MacMurray). 

He and actor Fred MacMurray were the only two actors in the cast that appeared in every episode of the full season. In subsequent seasons, his younger brother Barry Livingston took over the role of his adoptive brother Ernie. Livingston was born in the Californian city of Los Angeles. Later in his career, Livingston moved to Los Angeles and worked as a producer and director.
