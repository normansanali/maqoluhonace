Mandy Rose Makes Her OnlyFans Page Free For A Limited Time

Former NXT Women’s Champion Mandy Rose recently moved to OnlyFans and you can get a free subscription for a limited time.

The Golden Goddess recently announced she opened an OnlyFans page. Fans who act now can get a free membership to her page for a limited time.

Mandy Rose (Mandy Sacs) initially had an account on FanTime, which ultimately led to her termination by WWE. The venture was very lucrative, as the site revealed that in just one month of her joining, Mandy Rose earned over $1 million.

The former NXT Women’s Champion has since left FanTime and is now part of OnlyFans. Mandy Rose also tweeted a few photos giving a sneak peek on what the fans can expect on her website.

Mandy was released by WWE in December 2022 due to the content she posted on her FanTime page. Her release also signaled the end of her NXT Women’s title reign, which lasted 413 days.

In February, Mandy told Renee Paquette she was focused on other projects, but she would not rule out her return to wrestling. More recently, she answered fan questions on her Instagram Stories. One fan asked if she will wrestle again and while she didn’t directly answer the question, Mandy said she “may or may not be a free agent very soon.”
