Kanye West Releases Devil In A New Dress

Recommended Videos

For the latest G.O.O.D Friday, Kanye gives us a new track from his upcoming album. The new song is called ‘Devil In A New Dress’. For the first time in a while Kanye goes in alone on the song. He takes on all the verses and there are no guests on the track. I’m not a big fan of it to be honest. The lyrics are fine but the falsetto backing vocals are kind of annoying, the song is also too slow for my liking. Coming it at just under three minutes, it’s also a bit short. I enjoyed ‘Monster’, his G.O.O.D Friday track from last week, a lot better. Perhaps ‘Devil In A New Dress’ will grow on me though, who knows?
