Lisa Marie Varon Net Worth

Lisa Marie Varon, born on February 10, 1971 in San Bernardino, CA, is a two-time WWE Women's Champion who wrestled under the ring name Victoria. She later moved to TNA Wrestling as Tara, where she continued to make a name for herself in the wrestling world.

About

Two-time WWE Women's Champion wrestled under the ring name Victoria. She later moved to TNA Wrestling as Tara.

Before Fame

She was a professional Bodybuilder and while working as a trainer at the Crunch Fitness gym met the WWF Wrestler Chyna.

Trivia

Her entrance music during her wrestling heel (villain) days was T.A.T.U.'s "All the Things She Said."

Family Life

She was born in San Bernadino, California, to a Turkish-Puerto Rican family; all three of her brothers are amateur Wrestlers. She was married to Lee Varon from 1994 to 2015. She later started dating a man named Brandon.
