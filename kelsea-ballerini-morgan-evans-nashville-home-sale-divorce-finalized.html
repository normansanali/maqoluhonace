Kelsea Ballerini &amp; Morgan Evans find buyer for $4M Nashville home featuring four garden patios after

KELSEA Ballerini and Morgan Evans have found a buyer for their $3,950,000 Nashville home after finalizing their divorce on October 24.

The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal the country star's former home, featuring two outdoor patios, is under contract after being on the market for only 18 days.

Kelsea, 29, and Morgan, 37, listed their Tennessee home for sale on October 21, three days before finalizing their divorce.

After buying the home for $2.4M back in 2020, according to Zillow, the split couple will make a $1.5M profit on the Tudor-style property.

Nestled inside a gated community in one of Nashville's celebrity-packed neighborhoods, Kelsea's former home offers spacious six bedrooms and seven bathrooms.

The master bedroom suite features a marble bathroom, an outdoor and indoor shower, and a whopping three walk-in closets.

Kelsea put a lot of thought into her unique home design.

Many of the rooms include brightly patterned wall-papers in green, pink, blue and neutral shades.

The backyard boasts a massive garden with four garden patios for the new homeowner to sit and enjoy the view.

One patio is enclosed with glass walls and includes a large television and a fireplace.

The second patio is outdoors and a long 10-seater dining table fills up the space underneath a wooden awning.

A third patio is simple and has two chairs and a tiny coffee table set up to enjoy the garden view.

The forth outdoor seating area is located in the garden and has four giant wooden chairs gathered around an open fire pit.

END OF THE ROAD

Last week Friday, Page Six reported Kelsea and Morgan reached a settlement in their divorce.

According to documents obtained by the outlet, the filing stated the country music stars had a "prenuptial agreement in place and have divided up assets such as their Nashville home which is now 'vacated' and up for sale."

Kelsea and Morgan will split the $4M from the sale.

'DEEPLY DIFFICULT'

Back in August, The U.S. Sun confirmed Kelsea filed divorce papers in Nashville to end her marriage of five years.

In Tennessee court documents obtained by The U.S. Sun, Kelsea noted "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for divorce.

Kelsea first met Morgan in March 2016.

The duo was co-hosting the Country Music Channel Awards in Australia.
