Trish Stratus vs. Raquel Rodriguez Set For 6/19 WWE RAW, Updated Card

Trish Stratus will compete on the June 19 episode of WWE RAW.

WWE announced that the WWE Hall of Famer will face Raquel Rodriguez in a Money in the Bank Qualifying Match. The winner will join Becky Lynch, Zoey Stark, IYO SKY, Zelina Vega, and Bayley in the ladder match.

Plus, in a WWE Night of Champions rematch, Women’s World Champion Rhea Ripley will face Natalya. Ripley dominated Natalya and retained the title at the premium live event on May 27.

Elsewhere on the show, Matt Riddle’s feud with Imperium will continue, as he will face Ludwig Kaiser.

The updated card is as follows:

  • Matt Riddle vs. Ludwig Kaiser
  • Natalya vs. Rhea Ripley
  • Raquel Rodriguez vs. Trish Stratus
  • Bronson Reed vs. Shinsuke Nakamura
  • Seth Rollins’ World Heavyweight Championship Open Challenge
  • Logan Paul Returns

WrestleZone will have coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on Monday.
