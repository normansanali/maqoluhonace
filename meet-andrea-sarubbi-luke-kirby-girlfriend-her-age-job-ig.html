Meet Andrea Sarubbi, Luke Kirby Girlfriend

Actor Luke Kirby is currently dating costume designer girlfriend Andrea Sarubbi. They have been in a long-term relationship but never without the ups and downs along the path. They met each other way before the No Man No God actor was married to actress Allison Elliott.

Allison is best known for her works such as Living Dolls, Black Tie Affair, The Buccaneers, The Eternal, A Wrinkle in Time, Law & Order, 20th Century Women, and Lean on Pete among other movies and TV shows.

According to Live Ramp Up, Luke dated Allison, who is eight years older. They shared their wedding vows on 27 October 2012. But, unpublicized disputes plagued their marriage and they parted ways and got divorced eventually.

But, when Luke started filming Touched With Fire with actress Katie Holmes, the rumor of budding romance between the two ignited. DailyMail reported that he had “split” with Andrea and also moved out of the home they shared. All of this transpired amid speculation of a romance with Tin Cruise’s ex-wife.

They were reportedly spotted together several times during breaks on set. The off-the-set romance rumors were brushed off because he was dating Andrea at the time. A source revealed to Grazia magazine, “Luke is now a single man, which means that he and Katie are free to explore the feelings that they have for one another.”

National Inquirer reported that Luke was never gotten over his relationship with Andrea. They lived together until 2013 and a source revealed to the outlet that he still sounded like he was very much in love with Andrea.
