'Romper stomper' in Morgan Wallen porta potty brawl branded 'hero' for saving her mom

A brawl broke out at a Morgan Wallen concert this week and one girl, who has been nicknamed ‘romper stomper’, is being called a ‘hero’ after the video.

The fight erupted at the country music singer’s show in Pittsburgh on Wednesday night and the wild clip has gone viral on social media after the girl saved her mom.

Morgan Wallen porta potty brawl

The fight broke out at PNC Park, the home of the Pittsburgh Pirates, outside a line of porta potties at the Morgan Wallen gig.

A viral video begins with a brown-haired woman in a white cardigan standing in front of the red portable toilets holding one of the doors closed.

Another girl in denim shorts and a pink top then comes over and starts shouting and pushing her, but you can’t hear what she’s saying.

The door of another porta-potty swings open and she pushes the woman into the cubicle, physically fighting her as others gather around.

Girl nicknamed ‘romper stomper’

About halfway through the clip, a girl walks over who is dressed in a light blue denim romper and white cowboy boots.

She pulls one of the girls out of the portable loo and begins fighting her before returning to pull the other one out too.

Social media users have comedically nicknamed her ‘romper stomper’ thanks to her outfit choice and actions in the video.

She stomps her leg on the girl in the porta potty dramatically before calming down and going to grab some toilet roll from the cubicle.

Everyone thinks the nickname is totally hilarious, with one person writing on Twitter: “I’m cackling at the fact she’s getting called ‘the romper stomper’.”

A representative from Pittsburgh’s Department of Public Safety told Billboard there were no arrests, but “plenty of ejections for intoxication”.

They also said “the parties involved in the incident dispersed” before police were made aware of the fight, but anyone can file a police report “at any time”.

‘Romper stomper’ branded a ‘hero’

The girl, who is called Dalanie Disabato, revealed there was actually a heartwarming reason for her violence in an interview with Barstool Sports’ Dave Portnoy.

She explained that the brown-haired woman who was holding the door is actually her mom, and she walked into the bathroom to find her being tormented.

“This girl was pissed, she was mad,” she said. “So I hear this going on and I just walked out and saw two girls getting it up on my mum so I just did what I think any daughter would have done.”

“I had to do what I had to do. My mum was literally getting jumped in a porta potty. They were literally trying to put her head in the porta potty. I had to save her.”

Dalanie claimed she’s never been in a fight and didn’t know she was capable of doing that, but thinks the whole thing is “hilarious” looking back.

“The romper stomper!!!! Now knowing she beat this girl up for her momma makes me love this more,” someone wrote on Twitter.

Another agreed: “This is what happens when you mess with someone’s momma #romperstomper.”

Others are calling her a “legend,” “hero” and an “icon”!
