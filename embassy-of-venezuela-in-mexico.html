  • City: Mexico City
  • Fax: +52-55-5203-5072
  • Phone: +52-55-5203-4233,+52-55-5203-4435

About Embassy of Venezuela in Mexico

Venezuelan Embassy in Mexico City runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, Venezuelan, and international citizens in Mexico.

The Venezuelan Embassy in Mexico City supports Mexican citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding Venezuelan economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting Venezuelan Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in Venezuela
  • Want to apply and obtain Venezuelan Visa

Visa and Passport services

Venezuelan passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in Venezuela and Mexico.

The process of issuing Venezuelan Visa and Passport of Venezuela can take few weeks.

*Please contact with Venezuelan Embassy in Mexico City if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

Venezuelan representations in Mexico

In addition to the Venezuelan Embassy in Mexico City, Venezuela also has 1 other representation in Mexico:

  • Venezuelan Consulate in Monterrey

Mexican representations in Venezuela

  • Mexican Embassy in Miranda is the only Mexican representation in Venezuela

Venezuelan representations worldwide

Venezuelan Embassy In Mexico City represents one of 96 consular and diplomatic representations of Venezuela all over the world.

Foreign representations in Mexico

Venezuelan Embassy In Mexico City represents one of 201 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in Mexico.
