Steve Jobs' Daughter Eve Jobs Debuts As Model

We all walk through the legacy built by Steve Jobs, and that's true. Steve Jobs contributed to us in several ways throughout the years via science and technology that people around the world have been living a global life remotely.

With that, the Jobs family is a praiseworthy group. Recently, Steve Jobs' daughter Eve Jobs made her modeling debut.

Eve Jobs, who is now 22 years old, entered the modeling industry by posing for Glossier's holiday campaign featuring Euphoria's Sydney Sweeney and RuPaul's Drag Race's Naomi Smalls.

Jobs is photographed in a bubble bath with an assortment of gold jewelry. The picture features her applying Glossier's shimmery lip gloss as she has an eye mask.

Eve Jobs did not miss flaunting the photoshoot on her official Instagram.

She shared the image and captioned 'Biggest thanks to @emilyweiss & everyone at @glossier ! Go check out the collection' followed by a black heart emoji.

How Much Is Eve Jobs Net Worth?

With starting as a model, it's known that Eve Jobs will now accumulate her net worth from her modeling career. However, she is also an heir to her family's colossal fortune.

Eve Jobs' family currently has a total net worth of more than $21 Billion, as per Forbes, thanks to Eve's father, Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Company.

Besides that, Eve's mother, Laurene Powell, bought a minority stake of the NBA's Washington Wizards and the NHL's Washington Capitals. She also launched in 2016 and earned a substantial fortune.

Who Is Eve Jobs?

Eve Jobs is an Instagram star and a newly-turned model. She is widely acknowledged as the daughter of Apple Company's co-founder, Steve Jobs.

Eve was born on 30 April 1998 in the United States to her parents, Steve Jobs and Laurene Powell. She grew up with her three siblings, namely, Lisa, Reed, and Erin, in her family.

She is a graduate of Stanford University.

As an Instagram star, she has more than 162k followers on her official Instagram account.
