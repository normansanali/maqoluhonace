Andrew Hawkins Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Originally from Cleveland, Ohio, Andrew Austin Wyatt Hawkins (born March 10, 1986) is an American football wide receiver who plays for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League. He formerly played for the Cincinnati Bengals and St. Louis Rams of the National Football League, as well as the Montreal Alouettes of the Canadian Football League, where he was a member of the team that won back-to-back Grey Cup Championships in 2005 and 2006. From 2004 through 2007, he was a member of the Toledo Rockets’ collegiate football team.
