Nevina Shtylla - Age, Family, Bio

Television host and actress known for hosting the series E Diell on the Albanian network Top Channel. She was a pageant contestant when she was a teenager. She has earned more than 440,000 followers on her nevinashtylla Instagram account.

Before Fame

At 16, she won Miss Albania 2010. She said she did not expect to win until she heard her name called.

Trivia

In 2017, she appeared in the Albanian film You Can Call Me John. In 2021, she was in the Albanian film Love: Dashuri. She launched Nevermind sunglasses and eyewear brand.

Family Life

She dated a lawyer named Arbër Myftari.

Associated With

She and Kiara Tito are both famous Albanian television show hosts.
