Brock Purdy Salary &amp; Contract Details: How Much Does He Earn?

Brock Purdy’s rise from the collegiate ranks to the NFL ranks is admirable. He is now the quarterback for the San Francisco 49ers. He was selected in the seventh round of the 2022 NFL Draft. His four-year deal with the 49ers is for $3.737 million.

Brock Purdy Contract Details

Brock’s deal with the 49ers is for four years and is worth $3,737,008. With a $77,008 signing bonus, it’s clear that the organization sees promise in him. That same sum, $77,008, is guaranteed, bringing his yearly income to a respectable $934,252.
