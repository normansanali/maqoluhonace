What is Dennis Quaid’s Net Worth?

Dennis Quaid, the younger brother of actor Randy Quaid, has a net worth of $30 million dollars. He is best known for his roles in films such as Frequency, The Parent Trap, Vantage Point, and Great Balls of Fire.

Randy Quaid’s Early Life and Career

Randy Quaid was born in Houston, Texas in 1954 to parents Juanita and William Quaid. His mother worked in real estate, while his father was an electrician. Quaid’s older brother Dennis also became an actor. Quaid attended school in Bellaire, Texas, and developed an interest in dance and drama.

He pursued his passion for acting at the University of Houston, where he studied under drama coach Cecil Pickett. Quaid dropped out of university before graduating and moved to Hollywood to pursue a career in acting.

Dennis Quaid’s Career Highlights

Dennis Quaid’s career took off in the late 1970s with his role in the Academy Award-winning film, Breaking Away. He continued to gain traction throughout the 1980s with roles in over 15 movies, including The Right Stuff and Great Balls of Fire!. In the 1990s, Quaid faced personal struggles but continued to work and eventually landed leading roles in films like The Parent Trap and Any Given Sunday. He won awards for his work in Far From Heaven and portrayed former President Bill Clinton in The Special Relationship. Quaid also had recurring roles in several television series and starred in recent films such as A Dog’s Purpose and I Can Only Imagine. He was set to portray Ronald Reagan in a biopic before it was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Marriages and Personal Life

In 1978, Quaid married P.J. Soles and divorced in 1983. He then married Meg Ryan in 1991, had a son in 1992, and divorced in 2001. Quaid briefly dated Shanna Moakler in 2001 and later married Kimberly Buffington in 2004, with whom he had fraternal twins in 2007. They reconciled but separated in 2016 and divorced in 2018. Quaid dated Santa Auzina until 2019 and got engaged to Laura Savoie in the same year. They married in June 2020 in Santa Barbara, CA.

Quaid has been open about his past struggles with cocaine abuse in the 1970s and has since been an advocate for patient safety after his twins were given an accidental overdose of a blood thinner in the hospital.

Dennis Quaid’s Hobbies and Interests

Dennis Quaid is not only an accomplished actor, but also a talented musician. He plays in his own band, the Sharks, and has written several original songs which have appeared in his films. In addition to his musical pursuits, Quaid also enjoys golfing and claims a one-handicap. He is also a licensed pilot and enjoys flying.

In 2020, Quaid started his own podcast called the Pet Show with Dennis Quaid and Jimmy Jellinek. He is a devout Christian and dedicated the song he wrote for the film I Can Only Imagine to his mother.
