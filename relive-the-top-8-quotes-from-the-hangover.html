Relive the Top 8 Quotes From The Hangover

It’s been 10 years since Bradley Cooper (Phil), Ed Helms (Stu), Zach Galifianakis (Alan) and Justin Bartha (Doug) made their big screen debut together in the first Hangover movie, and Us Weekly is taking a look back at the best quotes from the funny flick.

The witty charm of the film’s characters — who take off to Las Vegas to celebrate a bachelor party when things go awry — quickly won the hearts of viewers, leading to two more movies in the franchise.

Whether it be Galifianakis’ completely absurd and raunchy explanation of things, including blaming Bin Laden for not being able to masturbate on an airplane, to Cooper’s dry humor, there are many quotable moments from the 2009 film.
