Biography

Flavia Palmiero was born in Argentina on July 11, 1966. Notable for her roles in the Argentine television shows Sos Mi Vida (You Are the One) and Son Amores (Sweethearts), she is also recognized for her hosting work on Alpiste Perdiste and Caiga Quien Caiga – CQC, as well as for her performances in the television comedy shows Flavia, Corazon de Tiza (1992) and Flavia (2003). She and Uruguayan actress and singer Natalia Oreiro were both in the cast of You Are the One. At the start of her career, she appeared in a 1985 episode of the television series Rosse.
