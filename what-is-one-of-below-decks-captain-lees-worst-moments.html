What is one of Below Deck's Captain Lee Rosbach's worst moments?

During an episode of Below Deck, a female charter guest pleaded with Kelley Johnson to go down the slide with them. As viewers, it was abundantly clear that Johnson was very uncomfortable with the idea of going down the slide with the guest as he firmly turned down the request. In response, the guest went to Captain Lee Rosbach to ask him to get Johnson to go down the slide with her and he gave in to her request. The Captain’s decision to give the charter guest what she wanted clearly put Johnson in an uncomfortable position. The guest even tried to get Johnson to take his shirt off but he wasn’t forced to do that.
