Lanisha Cole Husband, Ethnicity, Height, Nick Cannon

Model Lanisha Cole is a mother now. Yes! She welcomed her daughter on September 14, 2022, and has also revealed her baby daddy — it’s Nick Cannon!

Below you’ll find more information on Lanisha’s husband, ethnicity, height, and more.

Meet Lanisha Cole, Nick Cannon Baby Mama

Lanisha Cole started dating Nick Cannon in early 2017 (right after the rapper famously divorced singer/songwriter Mariah Carey). But soon in December of that year, Lanisha announced their breakup via Instagram.

The model then went on to cherish a private dating life.

However, fast forward to 2022, and to everyone’s surprise, Lanisha revealed her pregnancy (but without telling who her baby daddy was). And it wasn’t until a few weeks before the birth of her daughter on September 14, 2022, that Lanisha reveal Nick Cannon was the father.

On August 24, Nick also took it to his IG to share some maternity shots with Lanisha.

Anyway, now that Lanisha’s daughter Onyx Ice Cole Cannon is here, Nick’s overjoyed. He said, “I vow to protect, provide, guide, and love this child to the best of my abilities.”

Likewise, mama Lanisha also shared that she felt blessed to welcome Onyx into this world. “The joy that I feel as a mother is something that has forever opened up my heart in a huge new way. I’m already obsessed with her! And you already know there’ll be no shortage of pics and videos,” she added.

But while the parents were delighted, critics online questioned Nick’s untraditional views on monogamy. To which, the rapper responded, “I am not easily triggered and have quite tough skin and have always been an open book but not everyone in my family has that same level of strength.”

Furthermore, Nick pleaded with everyone to project all criticism and cynicism toward him and not the “loving and precious mothers of (his) children.”

And at the end, Nick also praised Lanisha mentioning that she was the “most guileless, peaceful and non-confrontational kind soul (he) ever witnessed.”

How Many Kids Does Lanisha Cole Have?

Lanisha Cole has only one kid, Onyx Ice Cole Cannon, whom she shares with Nick Cannon.

But though this is Lanisha’s first child, it’s baby no. 9 for Nick. And what’s more surprising is that Nick was expecting two more children before 2023! One with  Brittany Bell, and another with Abby De La Rosa.

Nick’s other eight children are twins Monroe and Moroccan with ex Mariah Carey; son Golden Sagon and daughter Powerful Queen with model Brittany Bell; twin sons Zion and Zillion with former radio personality Abby De La Rosa;  son Legendary Love with model Bre Tiesi; and son Zen (who died at 5 months old in December 2021 after being diagnosed with brain cancer) with Alyssa Scott.

Lanisha Cole Husband

Lanisha Cole was married to her ex-husband Rick Licht in December 2014 — after 10 months of their engagement and 6 years of relationship.

At the time, the couple’s marriage sparked quite a controversy after Lanisha called out an urban website for allegedly “mocking black women in interracial relationships and marriages while giving rappers and ballers passes all day long.”

In her open letter, Lanisha shared that calling someone a “sellout” based on the race of their significant other is “mean-spirited, pathetic and highlights everything wrong with our society.” Also, she confessed that she had never been in a relationship based on wealth or status. “If that was the case then I’m sure you would have seen me out and about with rappers, athletes, artists, etc. I’ve always stayed under the radar (by choice) and made my life decisions based on what I value, what my parents taught me, and what my heart tells me to do,” she explained.

Sadly, Lanisha then went on to divorce her ex-husband before 2017.

In case you want to know, Rick Licht was the “Chief Executive Officer and Board Member” of Hero Ventures, LLC ever since August 2011. Also, he was the founder of the Sports Law Department at the Beverly Hills Law Firm of Rosenfeld, Meyer & Sussman. Furthermore, created the Official All-Star Café restaurant chain with his clients and later formed the public company Ultimate Sports Entertainment, Inc., which transformed pro athletes into Super Heroes in retail and promotional comic books.

This UCLA School of Law has also dabbled in the entertainment industry working as a producer for movies like Chapel (2022), Prisoners of WAR – Subject Zero (2022), The Sinners (2020), Code_Switch (2019), and more.

Lanisha Cole Job

Lanisha Cole is a model who managed to go from being in music videos (at the age of 18) to spending 8 years on a well-known game show The Price Is Right. Also, she was a briefcase model for Deal or No Deal (from 2006 to 2008).

Besides this, Lanisha has spent over 9 years with Milani Cosmetics, opened an art gallery, opened her photography studio Lanisha Cole Photography, and also dedicated her time to mentoring young women who need guidance, among other things.

Furthermore, Lanisha has also dabbled in acting. She has graced Soul Plane, Fact or Faked: Paranormal Files, and A Beautiful Soul in 2004, 2011, and 2012, respectively.

“None of this came easy.  Nothing was handed to me.  I’ve faced the challenges time and time again, fighting to get to where I’m at today. I’m very proud to say that I’ve done it in a way that my family can be proud of,” she explained her journey.

Did you know: In 2010, Lanisha alleged that she was sexually harassed by producers Adam Sandler and Michael G. Richards while partially nude in a dressing room. The case was settled in 2013.
