Nevro HFX for Painful Diabetic Neuropathy

Nevro HFX is a non-drug treatment recently approved by the FDA for the pain related to diabetic neuropathy. In a clinical study, more than 8 out of 10 patients experience significant, long-term relief and 63% of patients saw a reduction in burning, tingling, and numbness sensations associated with their diabetic neuropathy.
