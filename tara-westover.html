Is An American Author Tara Westover Secretly Married? Know More About Her Relationship Status

The Audie Award-winning author for Autobiography, Tara Westover, is a well-known personality in the field of history and essay. She is best known for her book, Educated: A Memoir, which was awarded and nominated for many reputed awards and titles such as Best Book Of The Year, LA Times Book Prize in Biography, and Goodreads Choice Award.

Tara was born on 27 September 1986, in Clifton, Idaho, the USA, under the birth sign, Libra. Similarly, she belongs to white ethnicity and holds an American nationality. Not to mention, people are familiar with her career achievements; however, they are more curious to know about her personal life. So, let's take a peek in her matters too.

Besides her love life, she is a dog enthusiast as she has written it on her Twitter account. Moreover, she is also a book lover and often read novels and books and upload them on her Twitter account.

On SuperSoul Sunday Defining Love To Oprah

Recently on 4 May, she sat down with Oprah Winfrey for an interview on the show SuperSoul Sunday to talk about her past. In an interview, she explained about her dark past about her older brother abusing her.

She told that her older brother broke her wrist and pushed her head into a toilet, and she wrote all these things in her diary. One of her writing has, 

"It's strange how you give the people you love so much power over you." "We think of it as something that fixes everything, or, if the love is real, then you definitely have to stay in the relationship. The only way to really respect love is to respect its limits, and respect that it doesn’t give you the power to change other people.

Tara further added,

"That’s why you can love someone and still choose to say goodbye to them. It’s not necessarily a question of whether you love them, it’s a question of whether they belong in your life."

At that time, she was just 16-years-old.
