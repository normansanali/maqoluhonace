Shane Harper Biography-Wiki

Shane Harper who was born as Shane Steven Harper is an American actor, singer, songwriter as well as a dancer. Shane is well known for playing Spencer on Good Luck Charlie as well as Josh Wheaton in the independent Christian film God’s Not Dead. His self-titled debut album was released on February 14, 2012.

Shane Harper Education

Harper began dancing and acting at the age of nine and was involved in community theater and competitive dance. He also started singing and playing guitar and piano at the same age. He is trained in ballet, hip-hop, lyrical, jazz, and tap. He also studied martial arts between the ages of four and 12 and obtained a black belt in Karate. In the spring of 2006, a talent agent on the judging panel of a regional dance competition offered Harper an agency representation.

Shane Harper Family

Harper was born in La Jolla neighborhood of San Diego, California, the son of Tanya (née Michaelson) and Scott Harper. He is of Austrian, German, Jewish, and Scottish descent. He has an older sister named Samantha, also a professional dancer, and a younger brother named Sullivan, a model.

Shane Harper Wife

Harper was in a relationship with actress/singer Bridgit Mendler in May 2011. However, at the beginning of November 2015, Mendler officially announced that they had ended their relationship. Harper is a devout Christian.

Shane Harper Actor

Harper had minor roles as a principal dancer in various shows and movies such as High School Musical 2, Re-Animated, and Dance Revolution before becoming one of the lead dancers and the youngest (the Nick 6) on Nickelodeon’s Dance on Sunset. He landed on dance roles in 2006, including lead dancer for the music video Step Up with Samantha Jade, and Cartoon Network’s Re-Animated and the Saturday morning kid’s competition, Dance Revolution.
