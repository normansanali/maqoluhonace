Isla Fisher Seen Without Her Ring After Divorcing Sacha Baron Cohen Amid Rebel Wilson Scandal

Highlights

  • Isla Fisher seemed unbothered by recent divorce and looked calm and content as she walked her dog without her wedding ring.
  • The real reason behind the divorce was revealed: the couple was unable to agree on where to live.
  • Sacha Baron Cohen denies harassment allegations by Rebel Wilson, saying he has evidence that her claims are false.

Sacha Baron Cohen has been under scrutiny for the last few weeks, and not exactly for pleasant reasons. Rebel Wilson has publicly slammed him, insulting him and accusing him of threatening her to get her not to share her experience working with him. Shortly after, Isla Fisher announced that she was divorcing the actor, and obviously, fans assumed the separation had something to do with the accusations.

The couple shared their divorce news comes after Wilson opened up about working with the actor in her bombshell memoir.

Now, just a few days after the announcement, the actress was spotted in public for the first time, and she wasn't wearing her wedding ring. As she walked around with her dog, she looked very calm and content and had a phone conversation while holding her coffee to go.

Isla Fisher Spotted Without Her Wedding Ring In New Outing

In her first public appearance since the announcement of her divorce from Sacha Baron Cohen, Isla Fisher shocked fans by looking incredibly serene and content as she walked her dog around London. What drew the most attention was that, for the first time in years, she was seen without her wedding ring.

The actress seemed unfazed by the huge change in her personal life, having an animated conversation on the phone as she walked around dressed casually but stylishly. Amid the public scrutiny surrounding the divorce, Isla Fisher appeared to be embracing the moment with grace and positivity, despite the scandal surrounding her ex-husband, who was accused of harassment by Rebel Wilson in her recently released scathing memoir.

The couple announced their divorce on Instagram, saying "After a long tennis match lasting over twenty years, we are finally putting our racquets down."

Apparently, they have been separated for a few months, but they only revealed it recently. "In 2023 we jointly filed to end our marriage. We have always prioritized our privacy, and have been quietly working through this change. We forever share in our devotion and love for our children. We sincerely appreciate your respecting our family's wish for privacy."

The Real Reason Behind The Divorce

The divorce was announced after Sacha Baron Cohen was accused by Rebel Wilson of harassment, so a lot of fans speculated that the allegations against him had pushed Isla Fisher to end their marriage.

Considering that the divorce was filed in 2023, it's unlikely it had anything to do with Rebel Wilson's accusations, which the actor has firmly denied.

"While we appreciate the importance of speaking out, these demonstrably false claims are directly contradicted by extensive detailed evidence, including contemporaneous documents, film footage, and eyewitness accounts from those present before, during and after the production of The Brothers Grimsby," a rep for Sacha Baron Cohen said in response.

The real reason behind their divorce has finally been revealed, and it had nothing to do with Rebel Wilson. Apparently, the couple couldn't decide on where to settle down, even after 20 years together.

No matter how much two people love each other, such an important disagreement is bound to be a deal-breaker. If the recent pictures are any indication, Isla Fisher is dealing fairly well with the divorce.
