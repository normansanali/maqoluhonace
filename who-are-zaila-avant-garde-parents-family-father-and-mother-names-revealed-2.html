Who Are Zaila Avant-garde Parents? Family Father And Mother Names Revealed

Zaila avant-garde is the first African American to win the USScripps National Spelling Bee. Who are the teenager’s parents? Keep Reading to find out.

Zaila avant-garde is a 14-year old teenager from Harvey, Louisiana. She is currently all over the internet after she won the USScripps National Spelling Bee with the word ‘murraya’, a type of Tropical tree.

She spelled out words like ‘querimonious’ and ‘solidungulate’ in order to reach the final word.

The teenager explained how spelling is only a side hobby of hers as she is primarily focused on being a basketball pro and playing in the WNBA.

Zaila is a multifaceted individual as she holds three different world records for dribbling multiple balls at once in the Guinness World Record.

Who are Zaila Avant-Garde Parents? Mother And Father Names Revealed

Zaila Avant-Garde’s parents are Alma Heard and Jwara Spacetime, both of whom are from Lousiana.

Her father, Jwara Spacetime, was present with her during the spelling bee, encouraging her every step of the way.

Besides, she also has three brothers; Sol, Zwe, and Wonder.

More About The Spelling Bee Winner- Zaila Avant-Garde Bio

Zaila had always been a smart kid and her interest in world records began when her father gifted her a Guinness Book for her eighth birthday.

As she grew older, her interest in records increased and she started putting her plans into action.

At the same time, her interest in basketball also increased as she went on to set three world records for dribbling multiple balls.
