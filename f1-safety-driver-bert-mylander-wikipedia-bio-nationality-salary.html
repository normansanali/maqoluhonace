Who Is Bert Mylander? Wikipedia And Bio Details

According to Wikipedia, Bert Mylander, aka Bernd Maylander, is a current Formula One safety car driver from Germany. 

Bernd started out his professional career in karting at the end of the 1980s. He moved on to Formula Ford, Porsche Carrera Cup, and finally the original DTM in the years that followed. 

With a Porsche 911 GT3-R, he was able to win the 24 Hours of Nürburgring in 2000. The same year he raced for Mercedes-Benz in the Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters (DTM) series.

From 2001 to 2004, he competed in the DTM for Mercedes, having won only one race at the Hockenheimring.

Since starting out in 2000, Bert has collected over 21 years of experience as a safety driver in Formula One.
